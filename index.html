About WIN CoE

Fostering a Culture of Innovation & Enterprise

The Wadhwani Innovation Network Centre of Excellence at IIT Hyderabad serves as a strategic hub for driving innovation-led growth, entrepreneurship education, and societal impact. Established in partnership with the Wadhwani Foundation, WIN CoE integrates cutting-edge research, startup incubation, and policy engagement to address critical national challenges.

Core Pillars

Academic Excellence

The Centre leverages IIT Hyderabad's world-class faculty, interdisciplinary research culture, and advanced infrastructure to support high-quality research, proof-of-concept development, and technology readiness.

Industry Collaboration

Through strategic partnerships with industry leaders, corporates, and CSR stakeholders, the WIN CoE enables co-creation, sponsored research, technology validation, and commercialization of innovations.

Societal Impact

Aligned with national priorities and the UN Sustainable Development Goals, the Centre promotes inclusive entrepreneurship and scalable solutions that address critical societal and economic challenges.

National Alignment

WIN CoE actively supports government initiatives including Startup India, Atmanirbhar Bharat, National Education Policy 2020, and the National Innovation and Startup Policy, driving India's innovation agenda forward through strategic research and entrepreneurship programs.

Vision

To establish IIT Hyderabad as a premier innovation ecosystem driving entrepreneurship, technology commercialization, and societal transformation through research-driven solutions.

Mission

  • Nurture innovation-driven startups
  • Build industry-academia partnerships
  • Develop entrepreneurship capabilities
  • Strengthen innovation capacity

IIT Hyderabad

Established in 2008, IIT Hyderabad is a premier institution with world-class faculty and cutting-edge infrastructure. Ranked among India's top engineering institutions, IITH nurtures an entrepreneurial ecosystem at the intersection of technology, industry, and society.

Strategic Innovation Domains

Innovation Ecosystem

Translating Research into Impact

WIN CoE bridges the gap between academic research and real-world applications, supporting the journey from ideation to commercialization across multiple strategic technology domains.

Ideation & Validation

Identifying market needs, validating problem statements, and developing proof-of-concept prototypes through structured ideation workshops and mentorship.

Incubation & Development

Comprehensive support for technology development, business model refinement, team building, and access to laboratory facilities and technical infrastructure.

Scale & Commercialization

Facilitating market entry through investor connections, IP strategy, regulatory guidance, and partnerships with industry stakeholders for sustainable growth.

Research-to-Market Journey

Discovery & Research

Fundamental research, proof-of-concept development, and scientific validation leveraging IIT Hyderabad's research capabilities.

Prototype Development

Technology refinement, prototype iteration, and technical feasibility assessment with expert mentorship.

Validation & Piloting

Market validation, pilot deployments, user feedback integration, and regulatory pathway planning.

Market Launch

Startup formation, IP protection, funding facilitation, and strategic market entry with ongoing support.
